# How to Get a Free Key

<Warning>
  Before starting, any type of **adblocker must be disabled** to get it or you will **receive a timeout** for getting the key! <br />
  Make sure your executor **SUPPORTS Luarmor loader** or you will not be able to use nebora.org
</Warning>

1. Select a link between 'Linkvertise', 'Work.ink' or 'Lootlabs' by copying it from the discord or the script, after that head on to the website and click on "Start".
   ![help](/images/keysys1.png)

2. Click the `Get mspaint key` button.

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/2.png" />

1. Finish all required tasks on `Linkvertise`.

2. Once you finish the tasks, click the `Get Access` button.

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/4.png" />

1. After being redirected back, click `Get a new key`.
   <Note>If you already have a key, click the `Renew` button instead and skip to `step 9`.</Note>

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/5.png" />

1. Once the key is generated, copy it to your clipboard.

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/6.png" />

1. Open Discord and go to the [#get-script](https://discord.com/channels/1282361102935658777/1282373591652110417) channel and click the `Redeem Key`.

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/7.png" />

1. Paste the copied key into the text field and click `Submit`.
   <Warning>Ensure there are no extra spaces before or after the key, otherwise you see the `Something went wrong. Try again.` error.</Warning>

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/8.png" />

1. Click the `Get Script` button and copy the provided script.

<img height="450" width="450" src="/images/mspaint/keysys/9.png" />

![](/images/mspaint/keysys/10.png)
